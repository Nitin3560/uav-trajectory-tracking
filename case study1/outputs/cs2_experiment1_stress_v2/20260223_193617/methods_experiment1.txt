Stress semantics: traffic stress scales env.sensing.bytes_per_update and env.users.bytes_per_step by alpha; buffer stress scales env.comm.buffer_bytes_max; connectivity stress is injected as a comm-degradation fault window with severity (1-range_scale). Stress points: S0=(1x,1x,1x), S1=(2x,0.75x,1x), S2=(3x,0.5x,0.5x). Seeds were matched across policies (S0/S1: seeds 1-3, S2: seeds 1-10). The main IEEE figure reports S2 only (Static, Greedy, Agentic); supporting tables report S0/S1/S2 means and standard deviations.
